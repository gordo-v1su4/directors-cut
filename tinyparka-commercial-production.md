## Original reference prompt (Act I source)

```text
Style & Mood: Warm prestige American manor aesthetic. Cream walls, honey-toned hardwood floors, oil portrait paintings, brass table lamps, soft practical tungsten light. 50mm lens, shallow depth of field. Warm champagne grade. Deadpan comedy blocking — camera stays sincere, humor lives entirely in the performance. Medium wide, locked-off: the blonde woman in the gold floral wrap dress stands in the manor hallway facing a butler in a black tuxedo, who presents a silver platter holding a raw steak with white-gloved formality. She picks it up, looks at it once, and tosses it casually off-screen left — the same motion as discarding a napkin. The butler does not react. CUT — Close-up, 50mm, static: her face fills the frame. Blonde voluminous hair, heavy gold earrings. She looks directly into the lens, jaw relaxed, and delivers a line with the matter-of-fact cadence of someone reading a grocery list. CUT — Wide stabilized tracking: she strides through a gallery hallway, camera tracking ahead of her retreating, oil paintings passing on both sides. She is mid-anecdote. She does not look at the camera. CUT — Wide, slow dolly-in: she enters a warm study. A pink velvet wingback chair faces camera beside a brass floor lamp. She crosses and sits — crosses one leg, settles back, looks at camera. Completely still. CUT — Wide, locked-off from across the room: she is seated in the chair. To her immediate right, a large brown bear sits upright in an identical wingback chair, paws resting in its lap. She does not look at it. She continues speaking. Static Description: Traditional manor study — pink velvet wingback chairs, brass floor lamp, warm lamplight, oil paintings, dark wood bookshelves, Persian rug. The bear is well-groomed and calm. Neither occupant acknowledges the other.
```
